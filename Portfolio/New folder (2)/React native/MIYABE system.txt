Courses on the MIYABI system. MIYABI is a unique natural rejuvenation system with proven effectiveness. The main course is the Miyabi marathon. This is an intensive 21-day online training that includes a rich program, practice, and constant communication with the trainer. The marathon is conducted by Victoria Kushnaryova, the author of the Miyabi system, answering all the questions that the participants have during the development of the program. Where is the Miyabi Marathon? The training takes place online on the Marathon.Miyabi.Academy website, on a convenient platform specially created for this. Every day, except weekends, participants will be waiting for new techniques and exercises, as well as theoretical articles, for a deeper understanding of the processes with which they will work during the marathon.
