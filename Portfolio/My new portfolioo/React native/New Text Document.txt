UltaCart is a next-generation e-commerce marketplace platform for small local retail stores. We provide complete logistic, store setup and maintenance services, selling products online and promoting local producers or retail stores with their products across the entire country. Any small business can join our network independently or through our sales agent who is known as HBB, can list their products or services and can create an online store with just a few clicks. Millions of consumers visiting our site every day will buy your products and we charge a small marketplace fee which is lower than any existing similar platform or sites in the country. Our turnkey services will help you to focus on your core business and we manage entire logistic, supply chain and delivery on your behalf.
